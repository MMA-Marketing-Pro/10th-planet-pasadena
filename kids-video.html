About The Program

More Than Technique.

Our kids no-gi jiu-jitsu classes provide a disciplined environment for students ages 6-12. We introduce the grappling system of 10th Planet Jiu-Jitsu in a safe, fun environment with an emphasis on character building and good citizenship. Fundamental concepts are built upon in training and developed further through regular sparring in class.

Program Highlights
  • Ages 6-12
  • Discipline & citizenship focus
  • Regular live sparring
  • Led by Coach George Jáuregui, 1st-Degree Black Belt

Premier No-Gi Jiu-Jitsu academy in Altadena. Under the guidance of Erik "Compella" Cruz, 3rd-degree 10th Planet Black Belt.
