Track 01

Adult Fundamentals.

Every great grappler starts here. Our fundamentals curriculum teaches takedowns, submissions, positional control, and sweeping — the foundation for everything in the 10th Planet system. Rubber-Guard, The Lockdown, The Truck, and Leg Lock concepts are introduced early so you're speaking the 10P language from day one.

  • Submissions
  • Positional Control
  • Live Drilling

Track 02

Adult All-Levels.

Deep 10th Planet system work. Extensive drilling of transitional movement combined with advanced strategies in implementing techniques. Takedown, technique, live drill, sparring — with an emphasis on precision and speed. Mastery of Rubber-Guard, Truck, Lockdown, and Leg Lock systems are major components.

  • Rubber Guard
  • Truck System
  • The Lockdown
  • Leg Lock System
  • Open Sparring
  • Advanced Tactics

Track 03

Nano Techs.

The first step for your child. Nano Techs teaches the basics of jiu-jitsu in a safe, fun environment with an emphasis on character building, self-confidence, and discipline at an early age. The class meets twice a week for 45 minutes — the perfect introduction to martial arts.

  • Twice Per Week
  • 45 Minutes
  • Character Focus
  • Safe Environment
  • Playful Format
  • Parent-Approved
